Our Story

Since 2015, Siam Moo Kata has been bringing authentic Thai-Korean fusion dining to Bangkok. Our unique combination of traditional Thai hotpot and Korean BBQ creates an unforgettable culinary experience.

The Moo Kata Tradition

Moo Kata, literally meaning "pork skillet," originated from the fusion of Korean BBQ and Thai hotpot. This unique cooking style uses a special dome-shaped pan where you grill meat on top while cooking vegetables and noodles in the broth below.

At Siam Moo Kata, we've perfected this art by sourcing the freshest ingredients from local markets and creating our signature marinades that blend Thai herbs with Korean flavors. Every meal is a social experience meant to be shared with family and friends.

Our chefs have over 20 years of combined experience in Thai and Korean cuisine, ensuring that every dish maintains the authentic flavors while meeting modern quality standards.
